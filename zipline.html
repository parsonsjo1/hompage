Ziplining

Equipment: helmet, sunglasses, and gloves.
Weather: sunny non-windy weather is the best.

What It Is

A zip-line is a long steel cable or rope that connects two points at an incline and suspends a harness, handle, or seat.
The purpose of a zip-line is to provide the rider with an experience that is both thrilling and terrifying.
There are many zip-lines around the world that very in length, safety, and speed, but, nonetheless, are usually a blast to ride.

What To Be Aware Of

A zip-line is one of the most fun attarctions around, but it can also be very treacherous.
Always be aware of your surroundings as you zip-line. There may be trees and branches along that path that can cause some bad whiplash if not avoided.
It is also a good idea to start out on a smaller zip and work your way up to the big zip-lines. Using this method will help you prepare the terrifying heights that are ahead.
