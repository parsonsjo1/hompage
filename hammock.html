Hammocking

Equipment: hammock, blanket, and an open mind. Weather: best at night when the stars are shining.

What It Is

A hammock is a canvas or rope netting that is suspended by two cords connected at two points.
The purpose of a hammock is to provide the user with a most delightful napping experience.
Most hammocks in the world are very comfortable and will cause the user to fall asleep if used for too long.

What To Be Aware Of

A hammock can be a dangerous piece of equipment. If one is not careful they may fall asleep and end up as a bear snack.
Always be aware of where you are hanging your hammock. Make sure that it is a nice shady spot away from big bad animals.
Big animals are not the only thing to be on the look out for. One should be prepared to face the blood thirsty mosquitoes that are found just about everywhere.

Consider Bringing

  • A favorite blanket
    • For cuddling under the stars
  • A favorite friend
    • For snuggling of course!
  • A pillow
    • For taking nice naps
