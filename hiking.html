Hiking

Equipment: boots, walking stick, and strong feet.
Weather: best during early morning or later afternoon when it isn't super hot outside.

What It Is

Hiking is anything that includes a steep incline of doom that makes you sweaty and tired.
The purpose of a hiking is to explore the beautiful outdoors and to see mother nature, when she is not upset.
There are many places to hike around the world and there may even be one right outside your door, so go explore!


What To Be Aware Of

Hiking can be a dangerous adventure. There may be vicious snakes, lions, or mountain goats along the trail.
Be sure to be aware at all times while you are hiking because you never know when danger will strike.
It is a good idea to hike with a group or a buddy to avoid the unfortunate event of getting lost and being all alone.

Consider Bringing

  • A good pair of shoes
    • For all of those sharp, pointy stones
  • A waterbottle, full of water
    • For a thirsty friend
  • A roll of toliet paper
    • You never know
